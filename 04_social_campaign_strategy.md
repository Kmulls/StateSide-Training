# 21-DAY SOCIAL MEDIA CAMPAIGN STRATEGY

> A three-week countdown that mirrors the event's own arc: **Prepare the Body →
> Gather the Community → Align with the Planet.** Post daily, keep the visual
> language consistent (warm light, natural textures, hands and hearts), and
> always close with the same call to action and hashtag.
>
> **Primary CTA:** "Host or join a hub." · **Hashtags:** `#OurHouseActivation` `#RollingWave`

### 🌱 Week 1 (Days 1–7) — Prepare the Body: *Awareness & Invitation*

Goal: introduce the concept and the metaphysical premise; invite people to feel
curious rather than convinced.

| Day | Theme | Post Idea |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **The Announcement** | Reveal the date and the core equation. Short teaser video over the Unified Anthem. |
| 2 | **The Physical House** | Explainer carousel: how breath tunes the nervous system. |
| 3 | **The Why** | Founder / hub-lead story: why this activation, why now. |
| 4 | **The Rolling Wave** | Animated map graphic showing the event moving across time zones. |
| 5 | **Sound Spotlight** | Clip of a featured musician + how sound entrains a room. |
| 6 | **Breath Practice** | 60-second guided breath reel people can try immediately. |
| 7 | **Week 1 Recap** | Round up the week; pin the sign-up link for hosting a hub. |

### 🤝 Week 2 (Days 8–14) — Gather the Community: *Activation & Recruitment*

Goal: convert interest into commitment. This is the **14-day deployment mark** —
release the Master Deployment Checklist to confirmed hubs.

| Day | Theme | Post Idea |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 8 | **Meet the Outlet Families** | Spotlight confirmed regional hubs; humanize the network. |
| 9 | **Checklist Drop** | Publish the Master Deployment Checklist as a shareable graphic. |
| 10 | **Host a Hub** | Step-by-step: how anyone can host their own living-room node. |
| 11 | **The Boundary Grid** | Short demo of grounding the four corners (salt / sound / crystals). |
| 12 | **Audio Setup** | Practical reel: getting high-fidelity sound without pro gear. |
| 13 | **Testimonial** | Voice from a past gathering or pilot on the felt experience. |
| 14 | **Hub Roll Call** | Public map of every confirmed hub; celebrate the growing wave. |

### 🌍 Week 3 (Days 15–21) — Align with the Planet: *Countdown & Coherence*

Goal: build anticipation to the celestial peak and give participants everything
they need to show up ready.

| Day | Theme | Post Idea |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 15 | **The Planetary House** | Explain the solstice/equinox timing window and local solar noon/sunset. |
| 16 | **Find Your Local Time** | Simple guide to calculating your hub's ideal start window. |
| 17 | **Individual Prep** | Checklist reel: clothing, hydration, grounding access. |
| 18 | **The Anthem** | Full release / preview of the Unified Anthem track. |
| 19 | **T-Minus 2** | Behind-the-scenes of hubs setting up their spaces. |
| 20 | **T-Minus 1** | Final invitation; heartfelt "save your seat" call to action. |
| 21 | **Activation Day** | Live coverage of the Rolling Wave; reshare hub clips as the torch passes across time zones. |

### 📈 Content & Measurement Guidelines

- **Cadence:** One anchor post per day, plus stories/reshares of hub-generated clips.
- **Consistency:** Same color palette, same closing CTA, same hashtags on every post.
- **Amplification:** Each confirmed hub reshares to their local audience to widen the net.
- **Signals to watch:** Sign-ups to host, confirmed hub count, saves/shares (intent), and clips uploaded on Activation Day.
