# The "Our House" Activation — Core Concept

A distributed, community-hosted gathering that synchronizes people around the
world through shared intention, live sound, and celestial timing. Rather than
one central livestream, it moves as a **Rolling Wave** — each local hub ("Our
House") activates during its own daylight peak, passing the torch across time
zones.

The guiding idea:

> **Intentional Love × Celestial Timing × Somatic Release = Unified Resonance Field**

---

## The Three Houses

The concept scales the same act of care across three nested levels — body,
community, and planet — so the microcosm mirrors the macrocosm.

| House | What it is | The practice |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Physical (Body)** | The nervous system as a conductor | Breath and **fascia work** to release stored tension and open the body's capacity to send and receive energy |
| **Social (Community)** | Regional "outlet families" and global nodes | Entrainment — smaller, scattered groups organize around one shared harmonic signal |
| **Planetary (Earth)** | Solstice/equinox transition windows | Aligning collective intent with the Earth's natural turning points as an amplifier |

The intended outcome is **coherence**: when body, community, and planet move in
the same rhythm, the "static" of stress and separation quiets.

---

## The Event: A Rolling Wave

Each hub runs the same ~2-hour arc, timed to cross its **local solar noon
(11am–1pm)** or **sunset (5–7pm)**, ideally within a 2-hour window around the
exact solstice or equinox.

| Time | Phase | What happens |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 0:00–0:20 | **Arrive & Clear** | Neighbors gather; hub leads ground the space and hand out water |
| 0:20–0:40 | **Intention & Breath** | A guided group breath to settle the nervous system |
| 0:40–1:20 | **Sonic Imprint** | Live local musicians, or the shared "Unified Anthem" track |
| 1:20–1:40 | **Integration & Silence** | Stillness, then bare feet on the earth |
| 1:40–2:00+ | **Social Bridge** | Food, connection, and sharing clips to the next time zone |

## Hosting Framework (5 Steps)

1. **Anchor the space.** Clean and declutter the room, set the four corners with
   salt / sound / crystals, and set up a solid audio + streaming station.
2. **Sync to the cosmic peak.** Pinpoint the exact solstice/equinox minute;
   have hubs gather online ~30 minutes early to establish a shared baseline.
3. **Unwind the body.** Lead the group through **fascia work** to release stored
   tension and drop everyone out of fight-or-flight into receptivity.
4. **Amplify through sound.** Musicians perform a heart-centered anthem;
   already-synchronized nervous systems entrain to the frequency, and everyone
   directs the energy outward to Earth, community, and self.
5. **Ground and integrate.** Close with 5–10 minutes of silence, then step
   outside to discharge into the soil, and reconnect socially to share
   breakthroughs.

**14 days out**, share the hub checklist: clear and grid the space, use an
external speaker (not laptop/phone), set up a dual-device record station, and
prep comfortable clothing, clean water, and easy access to bare earth.

## Spreading the Word

A **21-day countdown** mirrors the event's own arc:

- **Week 1 — Prepare the Body:** introduce the concept, breath, and sound.
- **Week 2 — Gather the Community:** recruit and confirm hubs; release the
  checklist at the 14-day mark.
- **Week 3 — Align with the Planet:** explain the timing, drop the anthem, and
  count down to Activation Day.

Keep one anchor post per day, a consistent visual language, and the same call to
action — *host or join a hub* — throughout.
