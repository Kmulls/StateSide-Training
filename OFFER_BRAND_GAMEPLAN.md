# Our House — Offer, Brand & Gameplan

*A synthesis of the "Our House" Activation concept into a cohesive brand, a
layered offer, and a phased gameplan.*

> **North Star:** Turn a global moment of coherence into a durable brand — a
> movement people join for free, an experience they pay to attend, a practice
> they belong to, and a craft they get certified to lead.

The core concept lives in [`OUR_HOUSE_ACTIVATION.md`](./OUR_HOUSE_ACTIVATION.md).
This document is the business layer on top of it.

---

## PART A — BRAND CONCEPT

### Essence
**Our House** is a global ritual of coherence. Four times a year, on the
solstices and equinoxes, communities everywhere gather in their own homes and
spaces to breathe, release, and resonate together — a **Rolling Wave** of
presence passing across the planet, one time zone at a time.

### Positioning
> For people who feel the pull toward something bigger but are tired of doing it
> alone, **Our House** is a recurring, hostable ritual that syncs body,
> community, and planet — so a private practice becomes a shared, global one.

Not another app, festival, or retreat you fly to. It happens *where you already
are*, with *the people already near you*, on *the days the Earth itself turns*.

### The Three Pillars (from the Three Houses)
| Pillar | Brand meaning | What we deliver |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Body** | Come home to yourself | Breath + fascia work that releases tension and opens receptivity |
| **Community** | Come home to each other | Local "outlet family" hubs, entrained around one signal |
| **Earth** | Come home to the planet | Timed to celestial turning points; grounded back into the soil |

### Voice & Tone
Warm, grounded, and invitational — *not* preachy or hype. Confident about the
felt experience, humble about the mechanism. Speak in plain, sensory language
(warmth, breath, soil, sound) more than jargon. The move is always
**Invitation → Permission → Participation** — we open a door, we never push.
Every touchpoint ends the same way: **host or join a hub.**

### Three Modes of Engagement
People plug in through one of three doors — meet them where they already are:
- **Music — the Medium.** Sound is the carrier signal that imprints the moment.
- **Dance / Movement — the Participation.** The body joining in, not just watching.
- **Education — the Involvement.** Learning that deepens casual attendees into
  committed practitioners (the on-ramp to The Field and to certification).

### Sensory / Visual Direction
Natural light, warm neutrals and earth tones, hands and hearts, real homes and
real soil. Candid over polished. A single recurring motif — the **wave moving
across a map / across time zones** — as the signature visual.

### Naming Architecture
- **Our House** — the movement and master brand.
- **The Activation** — a single quarterly event.
- **Outlet Families** — local hubs / host communities.
- **The Field** — the ongoing membership container.
- **Hub Host Certification** — the facilitator training track, fully under
  the Our House name.
- **Unified Anthem** — the shared musical centerpiece.

**Tagline candidates:** *"Come home — together."* · *"One wave, one house."* ·
*"The whole world, at home, at once."*

## PART B — THE OFFER (Value Ladder)

One ecosystem, four rungs — each serving a different level of commitment and a
different audience, each feeding the next.

```
        ┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  T3 →  │  HUB HOST CERTIFICATION  (practitioners)      │  high price, few
        ├──────────────────────────────────────────────┤
  T2 →  │  THE FIELD — membership  (committed members)  │  recurring
        ├──────────────────────────────────────────────┤
  T1 →  │  THE ACTIVATION — ticketed event (seekers)    │  paid, quarterly
        ├──────────────────────────────────────────────┤
  T0 →  │  THE MOVEMENT — free to join / host (everyone)│  top of funnel
        └──────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### T0 — The Movement *(free · everyone)*
Anyone can **join a hub near them or host their own** for free using a public
starter kit (the event flow, the checklist, the anthem). This is the funnel and
the mission. It grows reach, seeds hubs, and makes every other rung possible.
- **Gets:** free hub starter kit + access to the live global anthem stream.
- **Goal served:** movement growth.

### T1 — The Activation *(ticketed · seekers)*
The paid quarterly experience. A ticket buys the **produced version**: the
live-streamed anthem performance, guided breath + fascia work sessions, the
digital program, and access to a hosted virtual hub for those without a local
one.
- **Formats:** virtual seat (low price) · local hub pass · premium/front-row tier.
- **Goal served:** revenue + willingness-to-pay validation.

### T2 — The Field *(membership · committed members)*
The between-events container. Members don't wait three months — they get monthly
practice sessions, the fascia + breath library, an outlet-family directory, and
priority/discounted access to each Activation. The quarterly peaks are the
recurring centerpiece.
- **Model:** monthly or annual subscription.
- **Goal served:** recurring revenue + retention + a warm base to seed hubs.

### T3 — Hub Host Certification *(training · practitioners)*
The training arm of **Our House**. A cohort-based program that certifies people —
bodyworkers, musicians, coaches, community leaders — to host official Activations
and run their own outlet families. Certified hosts become the distributed
delivery network *and* a revenue line.
- **Model:** higher-ticket cohorts; optional annual renewal / listing.
- **Goal served:** revenue + scalable delivery + leadership pipeline.

**The Human Capacity Curriculum** — the training's spine. Its premise:
*relationship requires capacity*, and capacity can be taught. It walks people
from **Survival → Maintenance → Flourishing** across three content areas:
- **Emotional Regulation** — self-regulation through somatic / fascia work.
- **Financial Literacy** — resources and the choices they unlock.
- **Caring for the Natural** — relationship with the larger living system you're
  part of.

### The Flywheel
Free hubs (**T0**) create seekers → seekers buy an Activation (**T1**) → attendees
join The Field to stay connected (**T2**) → the most devoted get certified to host
(**T3**) → certified hosts open more free hubs (**T0**). Each turn widens the base.

### Why People Buy
The experience is free to join, so the paid rungs sell **convenience and depth**,
not access. Along the **buyer's journey**, each rung removes friction the last one
exposed: T1 hands you a produced experience instead of a DIY one; T2 keeps the
practice going between peaks so you don't have to; T3 gives you the full
curriculum and credential to lead. **Convenience is the through-line of the
paid offer.**

## PART C — THE GAMEPLAN (Phased)

Anchored to the celestial calendar. *(Confirm each date/minute precisely before
committing.)*

| Peak (2026–27, approx.) | Phase focus |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Autumn Equinox — ~Sep 22, 2026** | **Phase 1: Pilot** |
| **Winter Solstice — ~Dec 21, 2026** | **Phase 2: Movement build** |
| **Spring Equinox — ~Mar 20, 2027** | **Phase 3: Monetize** |
| **Summer Solstice — ~Jun 21, 2027** | **Phase 4: Raise / partner** |

### Phase 1 — Pilot *(now → Autumn Equinox)*
Prove the concept works and *feels* right at small scale.
- Run **3–10 hubs** (your own + a few trusted "outlet families").
- Lock the 2-hour flow, the anthem, and the fascia + breath scripts.
- Capture everything: testimonials, photos, clips, and honest feedback.
- **Success =** hubs report genuine coherence; you have proof assets.

### Phase 2 — Movement Build *(→ Winter Solstice)*
Turn proof into reach.
- Publish the **free hub starter kit** (T0) and run the 21-day countdown campaign.
- Recruit toward a target hub count; build the outlet-family map.
- Soft-launch **The Field** (T2) to the warmest early participants.
- **Success =** meaningful hub growth + a first paying membership cohort.

### Phase 3 — Monetize *(→ Spring Equinox)*
Charge, and validate willingness to pay.
- Sell tickets to **The Activation** (T1) across virtual / hub / premium tiers.
- Open the first **Hub Host Certification** cohort (T3).
- Instrument pricing, conversion, and retention.
- **Success =** first real revenue across ≥2 rungs; unit economics understood.

### Phase 4 — Raise / Partner *(→ Summer Solstice)*
Package the traction for scale.
- Compile a **traction deck**: hubs, attendance, revenue, testimonials, reach.
- Court a **headline musician / act** and aligned **sponsors or partners**.
- Use the Summer Solstice as the flagship, partner-backed activation.
- **Success =** at least one signed partner/sponsor or artist; a repeatable
  playbook for every future peak.

> **What we're really building:** social *and* financial **infrastructure** — not
> just events. The hubs, the outlet-family network, the curriculum, and the
> recurring revenue together form the durable system the activations run on.

### Metrics to Watch Throughout
Hubs activated · attendees per activation · T0→T1→T2→T3 conversion · membership
retention · certified hosts · revenue per activation · clips/UGC generated.

### Integrity Guardrail *(read before marketing)*
This concept makes bold metaphysical and bodily claims. To keep it credible and
safe: frame benefits **experientially** ("many people feel calmer, more
connected"), **avoid medical or scientific claims** (curing disease, "healing
trauma," literal biological mechanisms), and add a light disclaimer that fascia
/ breath practices are wellness activities, not medical treatment. Sell the
*experience of coherence*, not a cure.
