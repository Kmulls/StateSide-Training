The shift

Our economy is built like silos. Life isn't.

Tall, vertical, separate — designed to store value in one place and defend it. But nothing alive grows that way. Under every forest floor, mycelium threads every root to every other, moving nutrients to wherever they're needed most.

That is the whole design shift in one image. Abundance was never accumulation. Abundance is circulation. To be abundant, we share.

The old design

Silos

Value climbs and stays put. Height without connection. Fragile, hoarded, alone.

The living design

Mycelium

Value travels the network to where it's needed. Low, woven, resilient — connecting all things.

The mission

Mend the sacred hoop.

The hoop is the whole — self, family, community, land, and the next generations. Our work is to help repair and grow businesses in a way that allows us to have deeper connectivity.

In sharing these tools and operational strategies, we can all grow — and share in the networking and marketing that's required for us to grow. This is a multi-generational concept.

We measure it in revenue streams, and in how we choose to look at how currency is traded amongst us. Money doesn't buy happiness, but it buys everything else.

The mechanism

Eight forms of capital.

Money is only one kind of wealth. A real economy runs on eight — and Our House is designed to grow and circulate every one of them. Explore the hoop.

The business arc

Connection → easy tools → community connectivity.

The model is an ecosystem. Each part feeds the next, and Our House provides the framework and the tech back end that hold it together. We understand that marketing and software is not everybody's cup of tea — but we will make it as easy as possible for you to integrate and put the tools to work.

01 · Gather

Live events create connection

People meet in real life — and everything taught and exchanged at live events can then be made into online and digital offerings.

02 · Weave

The tech stack keeps people linked

The back end captures contact information and lets people exchange connectivity, so a single night becomes an ongoing relationship instead of a memory. This is key to ongoing community building and revenue generation.

03 · Root

Connectivity becomes community

Sustained connection compounds into belonging — a vital family network that holds and grows with its own.

Our House provides the framework and the tech back end. The events create the spark; the stack carries it; the community keeps it alive.

It also allows businesses to continue to curate and create products and services for the people in the community — because often the easiest way to build something is to ask. When a community is healthy, they will tell you exactly what they want.
