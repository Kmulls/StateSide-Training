The back end

The tech is our oxygen.

Most of us who are in this work did not get into it to do the techie backend stuff — and we shouldn't have to. So we built a tech stack that is fast, adaptable, and simply part of the network's body.

Just as we need oxygen to breathe, our work needs its marketing to move — funnels, landing pages, checkouts — without anyone gasping for it. You bring the gift; the network carries the breath.

  • FunnelsThe inhaleA clear path from first hello to full participation.
  • Landing pagesThe doorwayEvery offering gets a warm, ready front door.
  • CheckoutsThe exchangeSimple, honest ways for value to change hands.
  • Follow-upThe exhaleThe connection continues after the room empties.

The visual model

We seed people, not just projects.

Here is the model we're building by: take a person with a gift, and seed them with three things — the tech and tools, the training and know-how, and the code of conduct we ourselves want to live by. What grows from that seed is the life we're actually after.

Each seeded person becomes a node who can seed the next — the network grows the way mycelium does: one living connection at a time. And in doing so, we create more chances to simply hang out with people like ourselves.
